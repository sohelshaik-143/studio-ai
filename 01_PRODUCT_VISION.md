# NEXORA AI
## Product Vision Document

## 1. Product Name

NEXORA AI

AI Skill-First Campus Recruitment Platform

Tagline:
Beyond Resumes. Discover Real Skills.

---

## 2. Vision Statement

To build the world's most intelligent campus recruitment ecosystem where students are discovered based on real skills, projects, potential, and learning ability rather than traditional resumes.

---

## 3. Mission

Nexora AI connects students, colleges, recruiters, and AI-driven assessment systems into one intelligent hiring ecosystem.

---

## 4. Core Belief

A resume shows what a student claims.

Skills show what a student can build.

Nexora AI discovers the difference.

---

## 5. Users

Students:
- Skill development
- AI career guidance
- Resume intelligence
- Job matching

Recruiters:
- AI candidate discovery
- Skill-based hiring
- Automated screening

Colleges:
- Placement analytics
- Student growth tracking

Administrators:
- Platform management

---

## 6. Future Vision

Create an AI-powered career operating system for every student.
