## Problem Statement Document

Version: 1.0

# 1. Introduction

The current campus recruitment ecosystem is built around outdated evaluation methods.

Most hiring decisions depend heavily on:

- Resumes
- Academic scores
- Basic interviews
- Limited assessment rounds

However, these methods fail to accurately identify a student's real capabilities, technical skills, creativity, and growth potential.

NEXORA AI addresses this gap by creating a skill-first, AI-powered recruitment ecosystem.


# 2. The Current Recruitment Problem

## 2.1 Resume-Based Hiring Limitations

A resume represents a student's claims, but it does not always represent actual capability.

Problems:

- Students exaggerate skills
- Recruiters cannot verify every claim
- Strong candidates may get rejected due to weak resumes
- Resume formatting affects opportunities
- Large applicant volumes create screening problems


## Impact

Thousands of capable students remain unnoticed because their potential is hidden behind inefficient evaluation methods.


# 3. Student Pain Points


## 3.1 Lack of Career Direction

Many students struggle with:

- Choosing the right technology stack
- Understanding industry requirements
- Planning career paths
- Identifying missing skills


## 3.2 Skill Gap Problem

Students often do not know:

- What skills companies expect
- Their current industry readiness
- Which areas need improvement


## 3.3 Opportunity Discovery Problem

Students face difficulty finding:

- Suitable jobs
- Internships
- Projects
- Learning resources


## 3.4 Lack of Personal Guidance

Traditional systems provide:

- Generic advice
- Limited feedback
- No personalized growth plan


# 4. Recruiter Pain Points


## 4.1 Candidate Filtering Problem

Companies receive:

- Thousands of applications
- Duplicate profiles
- Unverified skills

Manual screening becomes slow and expensive.


## 4.2 Skill Verification Problem

Recruiters struggle to understand:

- Can the candidate actually code?
- Can they build projects?
- Do they have practical knowledge?


## 4.3 Hiring Efficiency Problem

Traditional hiring creates:

- Longer recruitment cycles
- Higher operational costs
- Wrong hiring decisions


# 5. College Placement Challenges


## 5.1 Lack of Student Intelligence

Colleges often lack visibility into:

- Student skill levels
- Industry readiness
- Individual growth


## 5.2 Manual Placement Operations

Many processes are still:

- Spreadsheet based
- Manual tracking
- Time consuming


## 5.3 Limited Industry Connection

Colleges need better systems to connect:

Students → Skills → Companies
