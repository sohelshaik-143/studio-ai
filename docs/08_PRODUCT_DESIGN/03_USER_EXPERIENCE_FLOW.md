
NEXORA AI — User Experience Flow Document
# NEXORA AI

## User Experience Flow Document

Version: 1.0  
Status: UX Strategy Blueprint


# 1. Introduction

This document defines the user experience architecture of NEXORA AI.

The objective:

Create an AI-powered career experience where users always understand:

- Where they are
- What AI discovered
- What action they should take next



# 2. UX Principles


## Principle 1 — AI Should Guide, Not Confuse


AI output must be:

- Explainable
- Actionable
- Simple


## Principle 2 — Every Screen Has a Purpose


Every page should answer:



Why am I here?

What information do I get?

What should I do next?



## Principle 3 — Reduce Career Uncertainty


The platform should transform:



Confusion

↓

Clarity

↓

Action

↓

Growth
