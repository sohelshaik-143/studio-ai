# Career Studio OS

> **The Operating System for Career Growth**

---

## Mission

Career Studio OS exists to help students build trusted, evidence-based professional identities while enabling recruiters and educational institutions to make informed hiring and placement decisions.

Rather than replacing platforms such as GitHub, LinkedIn, or coding platforms, Career Studio connects them into one explainable Career Intelligence ecosystem.

---

## Vision

Create the world's most trusted Career Intelligence Platform that empowers every student to continuously improve their professional journey through verified data, explainable AI, and meaningful insights.

---

## Why Career Studio?

Today's hiring ecosystem is fragmented.

Students manage resumes in one place, projects in another, coding achievements elsewhere, certificates in folders, and internships across multiple platforms.

Recruiters spend significant time validating information from many disconnected sources.

Career Studio OS serves as the intelligence layer that securely connects these systems—with user permission—to provide a unified, explainable view of career development.
